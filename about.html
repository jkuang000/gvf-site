About Great Vows Foundation

Our Vision

Founded in 2025, our organization fosters inner peace, wisdom, and harmony by promoting meditation, Buddhist education, and compassionate service. We aim to integrate spiritual practice into daily life, cultivating mindfulness, ethical living, and interconnectedness. Through wisdom and compassion, we strive to create an inclusive environment and build a more mindful, peaceful, and enlightened society.

Our Focus Areas

1. Meditation and Mindfulness Promotion

  • Offer meditation sessions, retreats, and workshops to help individuals cultivate inner peace, mental clarity, and emotional resilience.
  • Provide structured guidance on integrating mindfulness into daily life, work, and relationships for personal well-being and social harmony.
  • Support research and innovation in meditation practices, exploring their benefits on mental and physical health.
  • Establish meditation centers and online platforms to make teachings accessible to a broader audience.

2. Buddhist and Ethical Education

  • Organize educational programs, lectures, and study groups that explore Buddhist philosophy, ethics, and practical applications for modern life.
  • Develop and distribute resources such as books, digital content, and online courses to promote wisdom, compassion, and self-awareness.
  • Collaborate with academic institutions to integrate Buddhist teachings into broader educational frameworks.
  • Offer training programs for teachers, practitioners, and community leaders to spread Buddhist values effectively.

3. Social Welfare and Community Engagement

  • Participate in charitable activities that address social issues such as poverty, homelessness, and disaster relief.
  • Organize volunteer programs that promote kindness, service, and social responsibility within communities.
  • Support mental health initiatives that incorporate meditation and Buddhist principles to improve emotional well-being.
  • Foster interfaith and intercultural dialogues to promote understanding and cooperation between diverse communities.

4. Environmental Stewardship and Sustainable Living

  • Advocate for a balanced approach to well-being by promoting spiritual, social, and environmental awareness.
  • Promote sustainable living practices, eco-conscious decision-making, and the ethical treatment of all living beings.
  • Engage in environmental conservation projects such as tree planting, clean energy advocacy, and reducing waste.
  • Encourage the development of eco-friendly spaces that align with principles of simplicity and mindfulness.

5. Holistic Education and Lifelong Learning

  • Academic and Leadership Development: Support Buddhist research, training, and leadership programs that cultivate scholars and practitioners in Dharma propagation and community service.
  • Public Spiritual Education: Organize public talks, meditation retreats, and cultural programs that make Buddhist wisdom accessible to all.
  • Compassion-Based Social Education: Enhance community service and outreach programs to foster ethical behavior, kindness, and a culture of care.
