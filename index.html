Great Vows Foundation

Our Mission

Our organization is committed to fostering inner peace, wisdom, and societal harmony through the promotion of meditation, Buddhist education, and compassionate service. By integrating spiritual practice into daily life, we aim to cultivate mindfulness, ethical living, and a deep sense of interconnectedness among individuals and communities.

Our Story

The Great Vows Foundation was founded with the vision of bringing timeless Buddhist values into modern life. Our journey began with a simple vow: to create a community where meditation and compassion can flourish, offering relief from the stress, anxiety, and disconnection so many face today. From humble beginnings, we continue to grow, guided by our commitment to help individuals discover inner peace and contribute to a more harmonious world.
